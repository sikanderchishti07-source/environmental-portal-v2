Our Methodology

Data Collection & Monitoring Process

A systematic, standards-based workflow from site mobilisation to compliance reporting

1

Project Scoping

Define monitoring objectives, regulatory requirements, receptor locations per NCEC/ISO

2

Site Assessment

Field reconnaissance, existing condition evaluation, background concentration measurement

3

Equipment Deployment

4

Sample Collection

Systematic collection of air samples, stack test runs, and real-time data logging
